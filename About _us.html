Welcome to the Jot-it Home Page

Mental health is very important in today's society. Even though mental health can not be cured by expressing your thoughts, it definitely helps. Expressing your thoughts is a great way to transfer ideas from your mind into words. Jot-it was created by a teenager in his parent's basement. The goal of Jot-it is to reach many users that suffer from mental health and Alexithymia.This website allows users to anonymously post what's on their minds. Respondes posted by users are called thoughts. Thoughts are saved in the user feed and can be manually edited or deleted over time. I really hope this site enables many useres to express thier thoughts in a postive way.
